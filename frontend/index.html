TradeTally - Free Trading Journal and Investment Tracker

TradeTally is a free, open-source trading journal and investment tracker that helps traders and investors track performance, analyze stocks, and improve results. Log unlimited trades with no subscription fees and take control of your data.

Whether you day trade stocks and options or build a long-term portfolio, TradeTally provides the tools you need to identify strengths, fix weaknesses, and grow.

Advanced Analytics and AI Insights

Visualize your performance with detailed P&L charts, win rate tracking, and risk analysis. AI-powered insights highlight patterns in your trading and suggest areas for improvement.

Investment Analysis and Portfolio Tracking

Go beyond trade journaling with the 8 Pillars stock analyzer, DCF valuation calculator, and real-time holdings tracking. Monitor unrealized P&L, track dividends, scan the Russell 2000 for undervalued stocks, and set price alerts -- all for $8/mo with Pro.

Open Source and Self-Hosted

TradeTally is fully open source and can be self-hosted with Docker. Your trading data stays on your own server, giving you complete privacy and control. No vendor lock-in, ever.
