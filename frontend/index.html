TradeTally - Free Trading Journal for Day Traders

TradeTally is a free, open-source trading journal that helps day traders track performance, analyze patterns, and improve results. Log unlimited trades with no subscription fees and take control of your trading data.

Whether you trade stocks, options, or futures, TradeTally provides the analytics tools you need to identify strengths, fix weaknesses, and grow as a trader.

Auto-Sync Your Broker

Connect your Schwab or Interactive Brokers account to automatically import trades. No manual data entry required. TradeTally also supports CSV import from Lightspeed, E*TRADE, ThinkorSwim, and more.

Advanced Analytics and AI Insights

Visualize your performance with detailed P&L charts, win rate tracking, and risk analysis. AI-powered insights highlight patterns in your trading and suggest areas for improvement.

Open Source and Self-Hosted

TradeTally is fully open source and can be self-hosted with Docker. Your trading data stays on your own server, giving you complete privacy and control. No vendor lock-in, ever.
